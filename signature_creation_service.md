# Signature Creation Service

## Placement in the eIDAS Trust Services Schema

| **Main Trust Service**                   | **Subservices**                                                                 |
|------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Electronic Signatures**                | - **Creation of signatures**<br>- Validation of signatures<br>- Preservation of signatures<br>- Issuance of signature certificates<br>- Management of remote signature creation devices<br>- Validation of certificates|

---

## Role and Importance of the Signature Creation Service

### 1. **Creation of Electronic Signatures**
   - This service enables individuals and organizations to create electronic signatures that are legally binding and secure.
   - It facilitates the signing process by using cryptographic methods to ensure the authenticity and integrity of signed documents.

### 2. **Integration with Qualified Signature Creation Devices (QSCD)**
   - The service often relies on Qualified Signature Creation Devices (QSCD) to generate signatures in compliance with eIDAS standards.
   - QSCDs ensure that the signature is created securely and that the private key used for signing remains protected.

### 3. **Support for Remote Signing**
   - The Signature Creation Service can be integrated with remote signing solutions, allowing users to sign documents from anywhere while maintaining security and compliance.
   - Remote signing is particularly useful for modern workflows that require flexibility.

### 4. **Legal Compliance**
   - Ensures that the created signature meets eIDAS requirements, such as being uniquely linked to the signatory and capable of identifying them.
   - Supports the creation of both Advanced Electronic Signatures (AdES) and Qualified Electronic Signatures (QES), depending on the level of assurance required.

### 5. **Cryptographic Security**
   - Uses strong encryption algorithms to ensure the integrity of the signed document and protect against unauthorized modifications.
   - Guarantees that the signature cannot be forged or altered after it has been applied.

---

## Summary

The **Signature Creation Service** is a foundational subservice within the **Electronic Signatures** category under eIDAS. It enables users to generate secure, legally binding electronic signatures using cryptographic methods and Qualified Signature Creation Devices (QSCD). By ensuring compliance with strict security and legal standards, this service supports trust in digital transactions across borders within the EU.
