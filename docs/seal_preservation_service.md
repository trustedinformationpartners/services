| **Main Trust Service** | **Subservices** |
|--------------------------|------------------|

---

## Role and Importance of the Seal Preservation Service

### 1. **Preservation of Electronic Seals**
   - This service ensures the long-term integrity and validity of electronic seals.
   - It guarantees that electronic seals remain legally effective even beyond their initial technical lifespan.

### 2. **Legal Certainty**
   - Provides assurance that preserved electronic seals can be validated in the future, ensuring compliance with eIDAS requirements.
   - Protects against risks such as certificate expiration, cryptographic algorithm obsolescence, or breaches at Certificate Authorities (CAs).

### 3. **Data Integrity and Reliability**
   - Ensures that sealed data remains intact and unaltered over time.
   - Creates verifiable proof of existence for sealed information using future-proof cryptographic techniques.

### 4. **Compliance with eIDAS Standards**
   - Operates according to Article 34 of eIDAS, which specifies requirements for preserving electronic seals.
   - Monitors cryptographic techniques to trigger updates when necessary, ensuring continued preservation.

## Market Creation Opportunities

### 1. **Cross-Border Transactions**
   - Enables businesses to preserve electronic seals for use in international trade, ensuring legal validity across all EU member states.
   - Facilitates long-term trust in digital transactions, creating opportunities for global expansion.

### 2. **Industry-Specific Applications**
   - Development of specialized preservation services tailored to sectors like finance, healthcare, and legal services where long-term data integrity is critical.
   - Supports vertical integration of seal preservation into industry-specific workflows.

### 3. **Integration with Other Trust Services**
   - Works in conjunction with services like archiving and validation to ensure comprehensive lifecycle management for sealed data.
   - Opens new markets by enabling innovative solutions at the intersection of multiple trust services.
