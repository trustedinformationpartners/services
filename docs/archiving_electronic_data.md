# Archiving Electronic Data and Documents Service

## Placement in the eIDAS Trust Services Schema

| **Main Trust Service** | **Subservices** |
|--------------------------|------------------|
| **Electronic Archiving** | **Preservation of electronic data and documents**<br> |

---

## Role and Importance of the Archiving Electronic Data and Documents Service

### 1. **Qualified Electronic Archiving**
   - This service ensures the long-term preservation of electronic data and documents with legal validity.
   - Qualified archiving services are provided by Qualified Trust Service Providers (QTSPs). An example is Docbyte.

### 2. **Legal Certainty**
   - The service provides greater legal certainty and a reversal of the burden of proof for electronic documents.
   - It guarantees the long-term validity of digital signatures and other forms of digital information, ensuring they remain legally admissible.

### 3. **Data Integrity and Reliability**
   - Ensures that archived data remains intact and unaltered over time.
   - Maintains the authenticity, integrity, and readability of documents and data throughout their retention period.
   - Preserves the integrity of digitally signed and sealed documents in line with eIDAS and ETSI 119.511 standards.

### 4. **Compliance with Standards**
   - Operates according to the OAIS model (Open Archival Information System).
   - Follows strict requirements for qualified electronic archiving, including NF 461 "Electronic archiving system" certification.
   - Ensures compliance with national and international regulations, including the upcoming revision of eIDAS.

## Market Creation Opportunities

### 1. **Cross-Industry Applications**
   - Enables secure archiving solutions for various sectors including healthcare, finance, insurance, and government.
   - Creates opportunities for businesses to ensure compliance with legal retention requirements while reducing operational costs.

### 2. **Integration with Digital Transformation**
   - Supports the European initiatives for fully digitizing the economy and introducing the European Digital Identity Wallet.
   - Facilitates the transition from paper-based to fully digital processes by ensuring long-term preservation of digital information.

### 3. **Enhanced Security and Trust Services**
   - Works in conjunction with other eIDAS trust services such as electronic signatures and seals.
   - Opens new markets by combining archiving with intelligent document processing and data verification solutions.

## Summary

The **Archiving Electronic Data and Documents Service** is a critical service within the **Electronic Archiving** category under eIDAS. It ensures the long-term preservation and legal validity of electronic information, fostering trust in digital transactions. This service represents a significant advancement in creating a safer and better way for preserving and sharing digital information, supporting cross-border trust within the EU while unlocking significant opportunities for market innovation and business transformation.
