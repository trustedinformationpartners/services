## Role and Importance of the Validation of Time Stamps Service

### 1. **Validation of Qualified Electronic Time Stamps**
   - This service ensures the validity and integrity of electronic time stamps.
   - Validation confirms that the timestamp accurately reflects the date and time it was applied and complies with eIDAS standards.

### 2. **Legal Certainty**
   - Provides assurance that the timestamp is legally valid and meets all eIDAS requirements.
   - Ensures that time stamps can be used as evidence in legal disputes across EU member states.

### 3. **Data Integrity and Reliability**
   - Verifies that the data linked to the timestamp has not been altered since the timestamp was applied.
   - Confirms the accuracy of the date and time indicated by the timestamp.

### 4. **Compliance with eIDAS Standards**
   - Operates according to Article 41 of eIDAS, which specifies requirements for validating electronic time stamps.
   - Follows ETSI TS 119 102-1 standards for verifying qualified electronic time stamps.
