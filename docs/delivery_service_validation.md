# Validation of Registered Delivery Service

## Placement in the eIDAS Trust Services Schema

| **Main Trust Service** | **Subservices** |
|--------------------------|------------------|
| **Electronic Registered Delivery** | - Registered delivery service <br>- **Validation of registered delivery service**<br>  |

---

## Role and Importance of the Validation of Registered Delivery Service

### 1. **Validation of Electronic Registered Delivery**
   - This service ensures the validity and integrity of data sent and received through electronic registered delivery services.
   - It verifies that the evidence related to the handling of transmitted data is authentic and legally valid.

### 2. **Legal Certainty**
   - Provides assurance that the registered delivery meets all eIDAS requirements, ensuring it can be used as evidence in legal disputes.
   - Confirms the legal validity of the proof of sending and receiving, which is crucial for time-sensitive or legally binding communications.

### 3. **Data Integrity and Reliability**
   - Verifies that the transmitted data has not been altered during the delivery process.
   - Confirms the identity of both the sender and the recipient involved in the registered delivery.
   - Validates the accuracy of the timestamps indicating when data was sent and received.

### 4. **Compliance with eIDAS Standards**
   - Operates according to Articles 43 and 44 of eIDAS, which specify requirements for electronic registered delivery services.
   - Follows ETSI standards for registered electronic mail and electronic registered delivery services (ETSI EN 319 521 and ETSI EN 319 522).

## Market Creation Opportunities

### 1. **Cross-Border Transactions**
   - Facilitates international business by ensuring electronic registered deliveries are legally valid across all EU member states.
   - Creates opportunities for businesses to expand into new markets through secure and legally recognized digital communications.

### 2. **Industry-Specific Applications**
   - Enables specialized validation services for sectors like legal services, healthcare, and financial services where proof of communication is critical.
   - Supports vertical integration of delivery validation into industry-specific workflows such as contract management, official notifications, or regulatory filings.

### 3. **Integration with Other Trust Services**
   - Works in conjunction with other eIDAS trust services such as electronic signatures, seals, and timestamping services.
   - Opens new markets by enabling innovative solutions at the intersection of multiple trust services.

## Summary

The **Validation of Registered Delivery Service** is a critical subservice within the **Electronic Registered Delivery** category under eIDAS. It ensures the legal validity and integrity of electronic registered deliveries, fostering trust in digital communications. By adhering to strict eIDAS standards, this service supports cross-border trust within the EU while unlocking significant opportunities for market innovation and business transformation.
