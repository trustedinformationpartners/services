# Remote Seal Creation Device Management Service

## Placement in the eIDAS Trust Services Schema

| **Main Trust Service** | **Subservices** |
|--------------------------|------------------|
| **Electronic Seals**    | - Creation of seals<br>- Validation of seals<br>- Preservation of seals<br>- Issuance of seal certificates<br>- **Management of remote seal creation devices** |

---

## Role and Importance of the Remote Seal Creation Device Management Service

### 1. **Management of Remote Seal Creation Devices**
   - This service ensures the secure operation and management of devices used to create electronic seals remotely.
   - It enables organizations to use remote systems for seal creation without compromising security or compliance.

### 2. **Legal Certainty**
   - Provides assurance that remote seal creation devices meet eIDAS requirements for qualified electronic seals.
   - Ensures that seals created using these devices are legally binding and maintain their integrity.

### 3. **Data Security and Reliability**
   - Implements robust security measures to protect sensitive data during the seal creation process.
   - Ensures that remote devices are tamper-proof and operate within a controlled environment.

### 4. **Compliance with eIDAS Standards**
   - Operates according to Annex II of eIDAS, which specifies requirements for Qualified Seal Creation Devices (QSCD).
   - Regular audits and conformity assessments ensure compliance with the latest cryptographic standards.
