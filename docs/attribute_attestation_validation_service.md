# Attribute Attestation Validation Service

## Placement in the eIDAS Trust Services Schema

| **Main Trust Service** | **Subservices** |
|--------------------------|------------------|
| **Electronic Attestation of Attributes** | - Issuance of electronic attestations of attributes<br>- **Validation of electronic attestations of attributes**<br>|

---

## Role and Importance of the Attribute Attestation Validation Service

### 1. **Validation of Electronic Attestations of Attributes**
   - This service ensures the validity and integrity of electronic attestations that verify specific attributes of natural or legal persons.
   - It confirms that attestations were issued by authorized providers and comply with eIDAS requirements.

### 2. **Legal Certainty**
   - Provides assurance that the attestation is legally valid and meets all eIDAS requirements.
   - Ensures that attribute attestations can be used as evidence in legal disputes across EU member states.

### 3. **Data Integrity and Reliability**
   - Verifies that the attested attributes have not been tampered with since the attestation was issued.
   - Confirms that the attestation was created using appropriate technical means and follows required data models.

### 4. **Compliance with eIDAS Standards**
   - Operates according to the requirements laid down in Annex V of the eIDAS Regulation.
   - Ensures attestations follow specified data models (ISO/IEC 18013-5:2021, W3C Verifiable Credentials) and enable selective disclosure of attributes.

## Market Creation Opportunities

### 1. **Cross-Border Recognition of Credentials**
   - Facilitates international recognition of qualifications and attributes by ensuring validated attestations are legally recognized across all EU member states.
   - Creates opportunities for businesses to expand into new markets through reliable verification of user attributes.

### 2. **Industry-Specific Applications**
   - Enables specialized validation services for sectors like education, healthcare, and financial services where verified attributes are critical.
   - Supports vertical integration of attribute verification into industry-specific workflows and compliance requirements.

### 3. **Integration with Digital Identity Ecosystems**
   - Works seamlessly with European Digital Identity Wallets, allowing users to present verified attributes with confidence.
   - Opens new markets at the intersection of identity management and attribute verification, enabling innovative solutions for secure and privacy-preserving identity systems.

---

## Summary

The **Attribute Attestation Validation Service** is a critical subservice within the **Electronic Attestation of Attributes** category under eIDAS. It ensures the legal validity and integrity of electronic attestations of attributes, fostering trust in digital credentials. By adhering to strict eIDAS standards, this service supports cross-border trust within the EU while unlocking significant opportunities for market innovation and business transformation in the digital identity ecosystem.
