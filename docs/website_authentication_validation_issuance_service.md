# Website Authentication Certificate Validation Service

## Placement in the eIDAS Trust Services Schema

| **Main Trust Service** | **Subservices** |
|--------------------------|------------------|
| **Website Authentication** | - Issuance of website authentication certificates<br>- **Validation of website authentication certificates**|

---

## Role and Importance of the Website Authentication Certificate Validation Service

### 1. **Validation of Website Authentication Certificates**
   - This service ensures the validity and integrity of certificates used for website authentication.
   - It verifies that certificates are current, have not been revoked, and were issued by trusted Certificate Authorities.

### 2. **Legal Certainty**
   - Provides assurance that a website's identity has been verified according to eIDAS standards.
   - Ensures that users can trust the authenticity of websites, particularly those using Qualified Website Authentication Certificates (QWACs).

### 3. **Security and Trust**
   - Checks the integrity of certificates to verify they have not been tampered with or compromised.
   - Protects users from phishing attacks and man-in-the-middle attacks by confirming website authenticity.

### 4. **Compliance with eIDAS Standards**
   - Operates according to the requirements set forth in the eIDAS Regulation for website authentication.
   - Ensures certificates meet the strict criteria for qualified status, including proper issuance by Qualified Trust Service Providers (QTSPs).

## Summary

The **Website Authentication Certificate Validation Service** is a critical subservice within the **Website Authentication** category under eIDAS. It ensures the ongoing validity and authenticity of website authentication certificates, fostering trust in online interactions. By adhering to strict eIDAS standards, this service supports secure digital transactions across borders within the EU while unlocking significant opportunities for market innovation and business transformation in the realm of online trust and security.
