# Seal Certificate Issuance Service

## Placement in the eIDAS Trust Services Schema

| **Main Trust Service** | **Subservices** |
|--------------------------|------------------|
| **Electronic Seals**    | - Creation of seals<br>- Validation of seals<br>- Preservation of seals<br>- **Issuance of seal certificates**<br>- Management of remote seal creation devices |

---

## Role and Importance of the Seal Certificate Issuance Service

### 1. **Issuance of Certificates for Electronic Seals**
   - This service is responsible for generating and issuing digital certificates used to create electronic seals.
   - Qualified certificates are issued by Qualified Trust Service Providers (QTSPs) and comply with strict eIDAS requirements.

### 2. **Legal Validity**
   - The certificates provide proof of identity for the entity **creating** the seal and ensure that electronic seals are legally binding under eIDAS.
   - Qualified certificates enable the creation of **Qualified Electronic Seals (QES)**, which guarantee the integrity and origin of sealed data.

### 3. **Integration with Remote Seal Creation**
   - The issuance service works seamlessly with remote seal creation devices by providing necessary certificates for secure sealing processes.
   - QTSPs ensure that certificates are stored in a Qualified Seal Creation Device (QSCD), enabling entities to create seals securely from any location.

### 4. **Compliance with eIDAS Standards**
   - The service ensures identity verification is performed before issuing a certificate, as required by Annex I of the eIDAS regulation.
   - Certificates must meet strict cryptographic standards to maintain their validity over time.

## Market Creation Opportunities

### 1. **Cross-Border Transactions**
   - Enables businesses to use electronic seals in international trade with the certainty that they are legally valid across all EU member states.
   - Creates new markets for businesses looking to expand into other EU countries by enabling reliable digital transactions.

### 2. **Industry-Specific Applications**
   - Development of specialized certificate issuance services tailored to sectors like finance, healthcare, and legal services where compliance is critical.
   - Supports vertical integration of seal issuance into industry-specific workflows.

### 3. **Integration with Emerging Technologies**
   - Potential to combine certificate issuance with blockchain, IoT device authentication, or AI-based verification systems.
   - Opens new markets at the intersection of trust services and innovative technology platforms.

## Summary

The **Seal Certificate Issuance Service** is a critical subservice within the **Electronic Seals** category under eIDAS. It provides the necessary digital certificates to enable secure, legally binding electronic seals. By ensuring compliance with strict identity verification and security standards, this service supports trust in electronic transactions across borders within the EU while simultaneously creating significant opportunities for market innovation and business transformation.
