# Seal Validation Service

## Placement in the eIDAS Trust Services Schema

| **Main Trust Service** | **Subservices** |
|--------------------------|------------------|
| **Electronic Seals**    | - Creation of seals<br>- **Validation of seals**<br>- Preservation of seals<br>- Issuance of seal certificates<br>- Management of remote seal creation devices |

---

## Role and Importance of the Seal Validation Service

### 1. **Validation of Electronic Seals**
   - This service ensures the validity and integrity of electronic seals.
   - Validation confirms the legal validity of a seal, which is distinct from simply verifying its technical properties.

### 2. **Legal Certainty**
   - Provides assurance that an electronic seal complies with eIDAS requirements, ensuring it can be used as evidence in legal disputes.
   - Qualified validation services confirm compliance through verification by a Qualified Trust Service Provider (QTSP).

### 3. **Data Integrity and Reliability**
   - Ensures that sealed data has not been tampered with since the seal was applied.
   - Verifies that the unique data set representing the sealing entity in the certificate is accurately provided to the relying party.
   - Confirms that the seal was created using a Qualified Electronic Seal Creation Device (QESCD).

### 4. **Compliance with eIDAS Standards**
   - Validates seals according to Article 32 of eIDAS, which outlines specific requirements for electronic seal validation.
   - Adheres to ETSI TS 119 102-1 standards, which define how to verify an electronic seal.

## Market Creation Opportunities

### 1. **Cross-Border Transactions**
   - Facilitates international trade by ensuring electronic seals are legally valid across all EU member states.
   - Creates opportunities for businesses to expand into new markets through secure and reliable digital transactions.

### 2. **Industry-Specific Applications**
   - Enables development of specialized validation services tailored to sectors like finance, healthcare, and legal services, where compliance is critical.
   - Supports vertical integration of seal validation into industry-specific workflows.

### 3. **Integration with Other Trust Services**
   - Works in conjunction with other eIDAS trust services such as timestamping and electronic delivery services.
   - Opens new markets by enabling innovative solutions at the intersection of multiple trust services.

## Summary

The **Seal Validation Service** is a critical subservice within the **Electronic Seals** category under eIDAS. It ensures the legal validity and integrity of electronic seals, fostering trust in digital transactions. By adhering to strict eIDAS standards, this service supports cross-border trust within the EU and unlocks significant opportunities for market innovation and business transformation.
